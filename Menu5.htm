Diamonds

Do not be fooled by the hacks or the apps with which you earn money to then exchange for diamonds. There are only two options, or you pay for them, or you get gift codes.

How to get them?

You have several ways, although the easiest is to pay for them:

Ø  Buy them

Buy or activate the Diamond Card so you are going to be able to collect diamonds daily. Obviously, the Diamond Card has a cost, but it is the easiest method.

Ø  Codes

They are not very common, usually, expire quickly, and they generally work for certain geographical areas. But of course if you find them, they are great and it’s the most realistic way to get free diamonds..
